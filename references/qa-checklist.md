# QA Checklist

## Content

- No stale sections from deleted topics remain in slide text, notes, or hidden copied content.
- Each slide has one clear message.
- Claims, dates, screenshots, logos, metrics, and diagrams trace back to user-provided or verified sources.
- Module names and person names are consistent.
- Chinese punctuation, dates, and numbering are consistent.

## Visual

- Text does not overflow, clip, or collide with shapes.
- All body text is readable at presentation size.
- Headings are aligned and use a consistent scale.
- Card spacing and margins are consistent.
- Images are not stretched, pixelated, dark, or cropped in a misleading way.
- Screenshots are large enough to read and have callouts when needed.
- Charts have labels, units, and a clear takeaway.

## PPTX Integrity

- Final output is a non-empty `.pptx`.
- Slides are editable native elements where practical.
- Template-following decks preserve source typography, spacing, page markers, and layout unless intentionally changed.
- Render final slides when possible and inspect key pages at full size.
- If render fails because of imported template image compatibility, still verify text structure, slide count, exported PPTX integrity, and any renderable key slides.

## Delivery

- Return the final PPTX path.
- Summarize only the most important edits or created sections.
- Mention any remaining caveat plainly.
